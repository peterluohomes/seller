The 5-Part Framework

Why every template works

Every template above follows the same skeleton. The substance changes; the structure does not. Understanding the why behind each part is what lets you adapt confidently when a real listing throws something the templates do not anticipate.

Universal Principles

  • Same day every week. Sunday evening or Monday morning. Predictability earns trust.
  • Always include the numbers. Never hide bad ones. Sellers detect padding instantly.
  • Always synthesize feedback into a pattern. Never just forward agent comments.
  • Always list specific actions. Sellers cannot see invisible work — name it.
  • Always close with one human line. The numbers fade. The human moment stays.
  • Calls should feel like they have a reason. Reserve them for moments that benefit from voice — overusing them burns out the channel.
